Throughout the years, the Ore Gangue Alumni have come together for various events, all with the same purpose - to celebrate our shared history and forge lasting connections. Whether it was called the Golden Jubilee, Homecoming Banquet, Anniversary Celebration, or simply a Reunion, the essence remained unchanged. It was a time for us to gather, to reminisce, and to celebrate the bonds that were formed within the hallowed halls of our alma mater.

The events themselves may have evolved over time, but the spirit of camaraderie and celebration always remained at the core. No matter when you graduated or where life took you, these gatherings were a chance to reconnect with old friends, share stories, and catch up on each other's lives.

Our generous alumni have always been instrumental in making these events possible. Through their sponsorship, we were able to offer a diverse range of activities, from campus tours and guest speakers to luncheons, banquets, poster displays, and alumni presentations. Their support ensured that every attendee had a memorable and enriching experience.

As we look back on the earlier years, we have shared some cherished photos that capture the joy and nostalgia of past reunions. For the most recent gathering, the 85th reunion, you can find the pictures on our [main reunion website](https://oregangue90threunion.square.site/) (scroll to bottom of the page).

We eagerly anticipate your presence at this year's event in May. Let us come together once again, to celebrate our shared history, to reconnect with old friends, and to create new memories that will last a lifetime.
