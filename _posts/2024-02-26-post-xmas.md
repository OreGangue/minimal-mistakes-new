


# Calgary Xmas Social
Attention all Ore Gangue Alumni and supporters of the Calgary Christmas Social!

As we gear up to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Calgary Christmas Social, we are reaching out to you, our cherished community, for your support in keeping this beloved tradition alive. The Ore Gangue Christmas Social, fondly known as the "Beer Bash", has been a cornerstone event for re-connecting, reminiscing, and creating lasting memories for four decades.

While the numbers may have dwindled over the years, the spirit and camaraderie of our gatherings remain as vibrant as ever. This year, we are in need of new organizers to step up and ensure that the 40th anniversary celebration is a resounding success. Previous organizers have retired, and Tracey, a key member, will be out of the country next December. Your help is crucial in carrying forward this legacy and ensuring that future generations can continue to enjoy this special event.

If you have a passion for preserving traditions, fostering connections, and creating unforgettable experiences, we urge you to consider volunteering as an organizer for this year's Christmas Social. Your dedication and enthusiasm will play a vital role in shaping the future of this cherished event.

Please reach out to [oreganguealumni@gmail.com](mailto:oreganguealumni@gmail.com) to express your interest in becoming a part of the organizing team. Let's come together to make the 40th anniversary of the Calgary Christmas Social a celebration to remember. Your contribution will be invaluable in keeping this tradition alive for years to come.

Thank you for your support and commitment to the Ore Gangue Christmas Social. Let's make this milestone year one to remember!
