2026 Golf Registration

Note: The Golf Committee is a volunteer organization and asks that you pay by e-transfer via your bank to simplify the registration process.

Entry form must be accompanied by the full fee. 

​

After you click on the Submit Registration button,  please take note of the information for completing an e-transfer.

 

Registration will be accepted on a first come first served basis.

​

REMINDER: Limit of 72 golfers
